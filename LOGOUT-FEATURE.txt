👤 FOR END USERS
─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

How to Logout:
  1. Look for your avatar in the top-right corner (circle with letter)
  2. Click on the avatar
  3. A menu will appear with your profile info
  4. Click the "Logout" button at the bottom
  5. Confirm when asked "Are you sure you want to logout?"
  6. You'll be logged out and redirected to the login page

That's it! Simple and secure.
