What is Retirement Planning?
Figure out how much corpus you need to retire comfortably. Save and invest enough so that your portfolio generates returns to cover your expenses forever. Once you hit your retirement number, work becomes optional — a concept also known as FIRE (Financial Independence, Retire Early).
💼 Work becomes optional 📈 Your money works for you 🕊 Freedom to choose your time
